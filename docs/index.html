PrivaMesh

A serverless, end-to-end encrypted messenger on Solana.

PrivaMesh is a private messenger with no backend of its own. It speaks only to the public Solana blockchain — no PrivaMesh server, no message relay, no account database. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted and your keys never leave your device.
